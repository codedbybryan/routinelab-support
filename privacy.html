Information We Collect

RoutineLab collects and stores the following information locally on your device and in your private iCloud account:

  • Workout data (exercises, sets, reps, weights)
  • Body measurements and weight tracking
  • Custom routines and exercise templates
  • App preferences and settings

How We Use Your Information

Your data is used solely to provide the app's functionality:

  • Track your fitness progress
  • Sync data across your devices
  • Provide personalized analytics and insights

Data Storage and Security

All personal data is stored in your private iCloud account using Apple's CloudKit service. We do not have access to your personal data. Apple handles all encryption and security measures.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal information with third parties. Your data remains private to you.
