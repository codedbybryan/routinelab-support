To sync your data across all your devices:

  1. Open RoutineLab and go to Settings
  2. Tap Sync & Data
  3. Enable iCloud Sync
  4. Make sure you're signed into the same iCloud account on all devices

Note: Initial sync may take a few minutes depending on your data size.

To show RPE buttons in your workouts:

  1. Go to Settings
  2. Tap Workout Settings
  3. Enable Show RPE

Note: RPE buttons will always appear for AMRAP and Cluster sets, regardless of this setting.

Try these troubleshooting steps:

  1. Check that you're signed into iCloud (Settings ‚Üí [Your Name])
  2. Verify iCloud Sync is enabled in RoutineLab settings
  3. Ensure you have a stable internet connection
  4. Force close and reopen RoutineLab
  5. Restart your device if the issue persists

If problems continue, contact support with your device and iOS version.
